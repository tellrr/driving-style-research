🔬 Research Note: Real-Time In-Cab Alerts are Deferred
Peer-reviewed RCT evidence shows detailed immediate feedback worsens driving by ~14.9% and reduces inter-accident time by ~7% (Msom 2020.0924; SSRN 3260891). Real-time driver-facing alerts are disabled pending resolution of feedback modality, frequency, and content risks. Manager alerts below are unaffected.
