Fleet Risk Distribution — Driver Tier
HistogramStacked Bar
Dimensions: X: risk tier (Conservative / Safe / Moderate / Aggressive / Dangerous) · Y: number of drivers · Colour fill: vehicle category (Car / LCV / Truck) · Tooltip: driver names per bar segment
Research basis: Five-tier classification from Driving Risk Assessment Using Near-Miss Events (Entropy 2021); four-tier K-means segmentation from Driving Behavior Risk Measurement and Cluster Analysis (MDPI 2023)
Audience: Fleet manager · Operations director
Harsh-Event Heat Map — Spatial & Temporal
Geo Heat MapTime Matrix
Dimensions: Map panel: lat/lng event clusters; intensity by density · Time matrix: hour-of-day (6 bands) × day-of-week · Filter: event type (braking / acceleration / cornering / all)
Research basis: GPS-Observed Exposure Metrics (TRB) showing road-type × time-of-day interaction effects; roadway geometry & grade influence (PubMed 34455341)
Audience: Fleet manager · Safety officer · Route planning team
Eco-Driving Performance Report
Multi-metric TableSparklines
Dimensions: Driver rows · Columns: top-gear cruising %, steady-speed %, idle time/trip, harsh-event rate per 100 km, estimated fuel waste vs. fleet benchmark · Sparkline: 4-week trend per driver · Fleet benchmark reference row
Research basis: ISAAC eco-score → 4–8% reduction in harsh-event odds per unit improvement (PubMed 38858043); ~5% fuel gap between best/worst driving styles (T&D 2022 doi:10.1016/j.trd.2022.103282)
Audience: Fleet manager · Operations director · Finance
