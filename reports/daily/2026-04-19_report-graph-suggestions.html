Intervention Impact Report — Before / After Coaching
Paired BarPre vs PostCoaching
Dimensions: X: behavior metric (speeding rate, harsh braking, harsh acceleration, eco-score gap) · Y: events per 100 km or score delta · Paired bars: pre-coaching (red/amber) vs. post-coaching (green) · Reduction % annotation · Groupable by individual driver or cohort · Time window: 90-day pre/post
Research basis: Web-based instruction reduced excessive speeding by 73.9% — Reducing Risky Driving (PMID 31847985); ADAS + coaching reduced harsh acceleration 76% and braking 65% — On the Impact of ADAS (PMID 36696744); feedback + incentives outperform feedback alone — Telematic Feedback and Financial Incentives (Science Direct S0001457521003092)
Audience: Fleet manager · Training manager
