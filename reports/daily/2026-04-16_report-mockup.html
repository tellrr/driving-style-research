Mass-Bias Risk: Why raw scores mislead in mixed fleets
A 40t HGV generates 0.28g for the same driver input that produces 0.48g in a 2t car. Without mass normalisation, HGVs are systematically under-scored and appear safer than they are. FleetLens applies per-class mass correction so scores are comparable across all vehicle types. The "uncorrected" column shows what competing platforms would report.
