Driver Safety Scorecard

Dimensions: Gauge: composite score 0–100 · Bars: CRITIC-weighted sub-scores (harsh braking, acceleration, cornering, speeding %, distraction) · Risk tier badge: conservative / safe / aggressive / dangerous + trend arrow · Line: 12-week rolling trend
Research basis: CRITIC-weighted composite with IQR thresholds — Driving Behavior Risk Measurement and Cluster Analysis (MDPI 2023); entropy-AHP five-grade segmentation — Analysis of taxi driving behavior and driving risk (IVS 2019)
Audience: Fleet manager · Operations director · Driver (self-service)
Harsh-Event Heat Map — Road Type × Time of Day
Heat Map MatrixFilter: Driver / Fleet
Dimensions: X: time-of-day band (Morning / Afternoon / Evening / Night) · Y: road type (Local / Arterial / Freeway / Rural) · Cell value: harsh events per 1,000 km · Color intensity: low=green → high=red · Filter: driver or fleet; event type

Audience: Fleet manager · Safety analyst
Fuel Consumption vs. Driving Style — Scatter
Scatter PlotRegressionConfidence Band
Dimensions: X: driver aggressiveness score (IDS, 0–100) · Y: fuel consumption (L/100 km) · Colour: vehicle type (Car / Van / Truck) · Dashed line: OLS regression · Shaded band: 95% confidence interval · Annotation: % fuel gap worst vs. best quintile
Research basis: IDS+PDF methodology quantifies ~5% fuel gap best vs. worst styles — Benchmarking driver acceleration impact on energy consumption (T&D 2022, doi:10.1016/j.trd.2022.103282); aggressive driving increases fuel 10–30% — Impact of Driving Style and Traffic on Emissions (SSRN 4012807)
Audience: Fleet manager · Operations director · Finance
Eco-Driving Performance Report

Audience: Fleet manager · Operations director · Finance
