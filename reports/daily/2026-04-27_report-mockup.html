Adaptive Threshold Engine
Vehicle-class parametric thresholds · Dual-tier severity (0.5 g harsh / 1.5 g severe) · Speed-band context · Jansen et al. empirical basis
v1 Active · 3 vehicle categories
Design Basis: Jansen et al. (2024) — A fixed deceleration threshold produces inconsistent harsh-braking detection across truck driving contexts. Nature Scientific Reports: driving styles are dynamic behavioral states, not fixed traits. Thresholds must be parametric by vehicle type and speed band.
HGV service braking exceeds 5 m/s² (0.51 g) under normal conditions (SAGE 2021). Geotab baseline: ~0.3 g for passenger vehicles. Dual-tier: 0.5 g = harsh, 1.5 g = severe (Data Mining of Telematics, arXiv 2304.10591). Weather/surface adjustment deferred — insufficient calibration data.
