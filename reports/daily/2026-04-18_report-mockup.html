Bayesian Fleet Self-Calibration
① Prior (new fleet)
Fleet-level average from all drivers. Score starts centred on fleet median. No country-hardcoded adjustments.
② Likelihood (each trip)
Poisson-Gamma update: each trip adds evidence. Score shifts toward true driving pattern. Credibility increases with trips.
③ Posterior (converged)
After ~30 trips: stable estimate. Score reflects driver's actual behaviour relative to own fleet distribution.
This approach self-calibrates to each fleet's own population — naturally adapting to cultural, regional, and vehicle-mix differences without hardcoded country adjustments. The Bayesian prior absorbs fleet-level baseline differences automatically.
